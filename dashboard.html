Key Security Principles

🔍 Think Before You Click
Always verify email senders and links before clicking. When in doubt, report it.
🛡️ Protect Your Access
Use strong, unique passwords and enable MFA on all accounts. Never share credentials.
📁 Secure Your Data
Handle customer data with care. Encrypt sensitive information and follow retention policies.
